About me

Driven by a passion for machine learning, artificial intelligence, and advancing technology, I’m a computer engineer with experience across applied research and development. I am committed to advancing the fields of model interpretability, multimodal data integration, and explainable AI, striving to make complex systems more accessible, adaptable, and practical for real-world challenges.

My journey has taken me through impactful roles, including a full-time engineering position at Google and a SWE internship at Google Research. These experiences allowed me to apply my skills in a variety of projects, from developing retail-focused AI solutions to creating model interpretability tools, all aimed at enhancing AI’s ability to generalize and perform in diverse settings.

Looking ahead, I’m eager to apply my skills toward developing adaptive AI systems capable of real-time interpretation across diverse data sources, particularly in healthcare. I’m passionate about creating AI-driven frameworks that empower clinicians with early, accurate diagnostics and insights. My goals include contributing to the field of AI with innovative, interpretable solutions that bridge technological and human needs.
